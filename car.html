  • Car tire managment app

    This desktop application was developed upon the request of a Serbian client who owns a warehouse for storing and a company for selling car tires. The project's objective was to eliminate extensive paperwork and store it digitally. Additionally, the client specifically requested a desktop application for ease of access. A crucial aspect of this application is data filtering, as various searches are frequent, both for tires and documents. Various types of documents have been created, and the entire application is designed to be straightforward, as per the client's request. Currently, the application is installed on 10 computers and processes approximately 7000 lines of data in the database daily.
